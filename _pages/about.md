# About Me

I am **Xiaoyu Ou**, currently pursuing a Ph.D. degree. I received my bachelor's degree from **Beijing Jiaotong University**.

My research interests include:

* Time-Series Analysis
* Semi-Supervised Learning
* Representation Learning

# Research Interests

My research focuses on developing machine learning methods for time-series data, with particular interests in time-series representation learning, semi-supervised learning, and contrastive learning.

# Publications

### Time-Series Contrastive Learning Against False Negatives and Class Imbalance

**Xiaoyu Ou** is the third author of this paper.

This work studies contrastive representation learning for time-series data, with a focus on reducing the influence of false negative samples and addressing class imbalance.
