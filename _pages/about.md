# About Me

I am **Xiaoyu Ou**, currently pursuing a Ph.D. degree. I received my bachelor's degree from **Beijing Jiaotong University**.

My research interests include time-series analysis, semi-supervised learning, and representation learning.

# Research Interests

* Time-Series Analysis
* Semi-Supervised Learning
* Representation Learning
* Contrastive Learning
